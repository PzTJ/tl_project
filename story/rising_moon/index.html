"...The "Hero License Public Examination"
will be held!"

A ceremony celebrating the 200th anniversary of the birth of heroes
will be held at the Archimedes Colony on the Moon.

On this milestone of a year, a special event has also been decided:
the "Hero License Public Examination" will take place!

To witness the moment new heroes are born,
Parallel Flight Inc., heroes from Archimedes, and even heroes from both inside and outside
the Earth Sphere have gathered in force for the ceremony!

However, just as the examination is about to begin,
your device starts showing a strange reaction...
and you're suddenly warped to another location!?

As you search for the cause of the warp phenomenon, you encounter:
a Boy in a Spacesuit Who Dreams of Adventures,
a Young Bounty Hunter who's staked everything on acquiring the license,
and even a Former Astronaut Hero who was there helping as an examiner!
And incredibly, a villainous combo of a Battle-crazed Bodyguard and an Antique Dealer!?

At this lunar ceremony, everyone's intentions and actions intertwine.
